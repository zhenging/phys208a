1303 Gravitational Potential Energy

For problems in which a body can be far from the earth's surface, we need a more general expression for gravitational potential energy.

The work WgravW_{grav}Wgrav​ done by the gravitational force when the body moves directly away from the center of the earth from r1r_1r1​ to r2r_2r2​ is given by

Wgrav=∫r1r2Frdr(1)\begin{aligned} W_{grav} = \int_{r_1}^{r_2} F_r dr \quad &\text{(1)} \end{aligned} Wgrav​=∫r1​r2​​Fr​dr​(1)​

FrF_rFr​ is opposite to the moving direction of the object, so FrF_rFr​ is negative

Fr=−GMEmr2(2)\begin{aligned} F_r = -\frac{GM_Em}{r^2} \quad &\text{(2)} \end{aligned} Fr​=−r2GME​m​​(2)​
