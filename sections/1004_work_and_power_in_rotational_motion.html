As the disk rotates from θ1\th_1θ1​ to θ2\th_2θ2​, the total work done by the torque is

When a torque does work on a rotating rigid body, the kinetic energy changes by an amount equal to the work done. The integrand of Eq.(3) can be transformed into an integrand with respect to ω\omegaω as follows:

Wtot=∫ω1ω2Iωdω=12Iω22−12Iω12(5)\begin{aligned} W_{tot} = \int_{\omega_1}^{\omega_2} I\omega d\omega = \frac{1}{2}I\omega_2^2 - \frac{1}{2}I\omega_1^2 \quad &\text{(5)} \end{aligned} Wtot​=∫ω1​ω2​​Iωdω=21​Iω22​−21​Iω12​​(5)​
