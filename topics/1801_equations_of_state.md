### 1801 Equations of State

#### Idea Gas Equation
$$
\begin{aligned}
pV = nRT
\end{aligned}
$$
$p$ - Gas pressures
$V$ - Gas volume
$n$ - Number of moles of gas
$R$ - Gas constant (For all gases, $R = 8.3144621(75) J/mol \cdot K$)
$T$ - Absolute temperature of Gas (Kevin)
