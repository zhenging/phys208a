(Mid Term 1) A crane operator lowers a $16000N$ steel ball with a downward acceleration of $3m/s^2$. Find the tension force of the cable.
>Solution
$$
\begin{aligned}
\sum F_y &= w - T = ma\\
T &= w-ma = w-\frac{w}{g} \cdot a\\
&= 16000 - \frac{16000}{10} \cdot 3 = 11200N
\end{aligned}
$$
