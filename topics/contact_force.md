Force $F$ is applied upon $m_1$ and $m_2$.
![Graph 2](../assets/contact_force.PNG)
a). Find $a$.
b). Find contact force $F_c$
>Solution
$$
\begin{aligned}
 \text{a. } F &= (m_1 + m_2) a\\
\To a &= \frac{F}{m_1 + m_2} = \frac{10}{1 + 2} \approx 3.3 m/s^2\\
\text{b. } F_c &= m_1 a \approx 3.3 N
\end{aligned}
$$
